An abstract type for vegetation models. Concrete types include a plant hydraulics model, but future types will include multi-layer canopy models and possibly a big leaf model.

source
ClimaLSM.PlantHydraulics.PlantHydraulicsModel — Type
PlantHydraulicsModel{FT, PS, D, RE, T, B} <: AbstractVegetationModel{FT}

Defines, and constructs instances of, the PlantHydraulicsModel type, which is used for simulation flux of water to/from soil, along roots of different depths, along a stem, to a leaf, and ultimately being lost from the system by transpiration. This model can be used in standalone mode by prescribing the transpiration rate and soil matric potential at the root tips or flux in the roots, or with a dynamic soil model using ClimaLSM.

  • parameters: Parameters required by the Plant Hydraulics model

  • domain: The plant hydraulics model domain, of type AbstractVegetationDomain

  • root_extraction: The root extraction model, of type AbstractRootExtraction

  • transpiration: The transpiration model, of type AbstractTranspiration

ClimaLSM.PlantHydraulics.augmented_liquid_fraction — Function
augmented_liquid_fraction(
    ν::FT, 
    S_l::FT) where {FT}

Computes the augmented liquid fraction from porosity and effective saturation. Augmented liquid fraction allows for oversaturation: an expansion of the volume of space available for storage in a plant compartment. It is analogous to the augmented liquid fraction state variable in the soil model.

Converts augmented liquid fraction (ϑl) to effective saturation (Sl), and then effective saturation to absolute pressure, represented by the height (ψ) of the water column that would give rise to this pressure. Pressure for both the unsaturated and saturated regimes are calculated. Units are in meters.

An extension of the PlantHydraulics.flux_out_roots function, which returns the net flux of water between the roots and the soil, when both soil and plant hydraulics are modeled prognostically. This is for use in an LSM.

ClimaLSM.PlantHydraulics.flux — Function
flux(
    z1,
    z2,
    ψ1,
    ψ2,
    vg_α,
    vg_n,
    vg_m,
    ν,
    S_s,
    K_sat1,
    K_sat2,

) where {FT} Computes the water flux given 1) the absolute pressure (represented by corresponding water column height) at two points located at z1 and z2, corresponding here to the middle of each compartment (for eg. a stem or leaf compartment), and 2) the maximum conductivity along the flow path between these two points, assuming an arithmetic mean for mean Ksat between the two points (Bonan, 2019; Zhu, 2008) to take into account the change in Ksat halfway between z1 and z2. The expression is given in full in the Clima docs in section 6.4 "Bulk plant hydraulics model".

Plan Hydraulics Parameters

ClimaLSM.PlantHydraulics.PlantHydraulicsParameters — Type
PlantHydraulicsParameters{FT <: AbstractFloat}

A struct for holding parameters of the PlantHydraulics Model.

  • area_index: RAI, SAI, LAI in [m2 m-2]

  • K_sat: water conductivity in the above-ground plant compartments (m/s) when pressure is zero, a maximum

  • vg_α: van Genuchten parameter (1/m)

  • vg_n: van Genuchten parameter (unitless)

  • vg_m: van Genuchten parameter (unitless)

  • ν: porosity (m3/m3)

  • S_s: storativity (m3/m3)

  • root_distribution: Root distribution function P(z)

  • earth_param_set: Physical Constants and other Clima-wide parameters

ClimaLSM.PlantHydraulics.AbstractRootExtraction — Type
AbstractRootExtraction{FT <: AbstractFloat}

An abstract type for types representing different models of water exchange between soil and plants. Currently, prescribed soil matric potential and prescribed flux models are supported for standalone plant hydraulics. Use within an LSM requires types defined within ClimaLSM, and include a prognostic soil pressure for models with both soil and roots.
